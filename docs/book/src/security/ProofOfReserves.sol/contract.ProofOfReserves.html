Title: ProofOfReserves

Author: AOXC Core Engineering

Real-time on-chain verification of asset backing vs total supply.

Integrates with Ledger and Oracle to provide a definitive solvency status. Optimized for Solidity 0.8.33 with strict adherence to Forge linting standards.
